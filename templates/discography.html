2010 was the year for Kokori's first release, the EP "init()", and after it the band kept working on new material, participating in more than 30 compilations.

In July 2013 that material took form of a new digital-only EP, "make install", which you can download for free.

This EP sums up an era, while installing the roots of great things to come...

...and the installation was fruitful, many bugs were fixed and the band was now ready for a "Release Candid Hate".

This new EP, released in August 2013, marks the first time kokori does an heavy bet on vocals and lyrics, and is released in vinyl, with a two discs special edition for those who want all the bits.

In 2014 kokori released a single, "Desert Moon / First Egg", in a limited edition 7" vinyl that soon sold out. But they were far from done yet, and preparing more material for...

Rootkit, kokori's full album, that was released in January 2016, and re-released in CD format in 2023.

A new album will be released in 2025, a split with Floating Ashes, and the debut of a series of records by ANTI-DEMOS-CRACIA.
